About Gear & Glaze

Gear & Glaze is the realization of a dream shared by the Houston family. It started with Isaac, a skilled mechanic with a passion for cars, and his wife, Dr. Lara, a botanist with a love for unique flavors.

Their daughters inherited their mother's talent in the kitchen and became expert pastry chefs, trained in France. This unique combination of passions led to Gear & Glaze - a place where the community can work on their vehicles and enjoy exceptional French pastries.

We are committed to providing a welcoming space for everyone, fostering a sense of community, and delivering the highest quality in everything we do.

Our Spicy Honey

Our unique spicy honey is crafted from peppers grown in our family garden. We carefully blend these peppers with high-quality, locally sourced honey using traditional methods.

The result is a delicious and versatile condiment that adds a touch of sweetness and heat to any dish.
